Privacy Policy

App: CHAWKPRO TRACKER v1.0  ·  Last updated: April 2026
TL;DR CHAWKPRO TRACKER stores all your data locally on your device only. We collect nothing, transmit nothing, and share nothing. Your business data is entirely yours.

1. Data Collection

CHAWKPRO TRACKER does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information you enter — including sales records, stock items, debt records, and user PINs — is stored exclusively on your device using your browser's local storage and IndexedDB. No data ever leaves your device.

2. No Internet Required

The app is designed to function fully offline. No data is sent to any server, cloud service, database, or third party at any time, whether you are online or offline.

3. No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use analytics tools, advertising SDKs, cookies, tracking pixels, fingerprinting, or any form of user monitoring. We have no visibility into how you use the app.

4. Permissions Used

The app does not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, SMS, or any sensitive device permissions.

✓ No Camera ✓ No Location ✓ No Contacts ✓ No Microphone ✓ No SMS Access

5. Data Export & Backup

The app includes an export feature that saves your data as a JSON file directly to your device. This file is under your full control and is never uploaded to any server. You may import this file to restore data on any device.

6. Third-Party Services

CHAWKPRO TRACKER does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, payment processors, or external platforms. The app is entirely self-contained.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for business use by adults. It is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the new version will be published at this URL with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
